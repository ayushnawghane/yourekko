Our Story

In 2020, Founder and CEO Zach Spring was diagnosed with b-cell lymphoma, brain tumors. The thought of death was not as concerning as the thought of leaving his loved ones behind to mourn with only old memories, pictures, and videos. One year later, he lost his uncle to cancer and then a close friend too. These experiences made him realize, we need a middle ground between nothing and Trusts or Wills which someone has to carry out whenever that day comes - so YourEkko was born.

From his cancer experience, Zach teamed up with Ohio State's Comprehensive Cancer Center – James Cancer Hospital and Solove Research Institute, where he was treated, to support the vision of a cancer-free world! YourEkko donates monthly, per Ekkos plan purchase, and any unclaimed money for Recipients. If you would like to contribute directly to the OSUCCC – James, you can do so by clicking the link below.

We care for others and express our love and gratitude while we're alive, why not do so after we're gone?

- Zach Spring

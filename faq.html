Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your most pressing questions about YourEkko and how it works.

YourEkko is a platform that allows you to create meaningful posthumous messages for your loved ones. With our service, you can schedule these heartfelt messages to be sent at the right moment. It's a way to keep connections alive, even after parting.

Creating Ekkoes is simple! Just sign up, choose a message type, and personalize it. You can schedule it for any future date, ensuring your sentiments reach your loved ones when it matters most.

Ekkoes are heartfelt messages you create for your loved ones to be sent posthumously. They can include personal stories, memories, or simply expressions of love. Each Ekko is a unique way to honor and remember.

YourEkko offers various subscription plans, including a free tier to get started. Paid plans provide additional features and benefits. You can choose the plan that best fits your needs.
